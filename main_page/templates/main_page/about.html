Our Story

Delicious Pizza has been a cornerstone of our community for over 20 years. Founded by the passionate Chef Hayk, our pizzeria has always prioritized quality, taste, and a warm atmosphere.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create the best pizza in town using the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes or sell the best pizzas in town. We believe that good food brings people together, and we strive to make every dining experience memorable.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible.
  • Community: We are proud to be a part of our local community and support local events and initiatives.
  • Tradition: Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic taste of Italy.
  • Innovation: While we respect tradition, we are always exploring new flavors and techniques to delight our customers.

Meet the Team

Our team is like a family. From our skilled chefs to our friendly waitstaff, every member of the Delicious Pizza team is dedicated to making your visit exceptional. Come meet us and experience the difference that passion and commitment make.
